# Protocol v1 — LPS-AD / PVO study

## Objective
Evaluate anti-neuroinflammatory effect of Plukenetia volubilis seed oil (PVO) against
Galectin-3 (Gal-3) in an LPS-induced neuroinflammation model of Alzheimer's disease.

## Animals & groups
Adult male Sprague-Dawley rats. n = 8 per group.
1. Control (vehicle)
2. LPS only
3. LPS + PVO low dose
4. LPS + PVO high dose

## Induction
LPS administered intraperitoneally (i.p.) at **1 mg/kg**, once daily for 7 days.

## Behavioral testing
Morris Water Maze (MWM): 5 acquisition days + probe trial.
Novel Object Recognition (NOR): familiarization + test (24 h delay).

## Endpoints
ELISA: Gal-3, p-Tau (hippocampal lysate).
Histology: H&E (hippocampus, cortex).
