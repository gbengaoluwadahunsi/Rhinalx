In vivo run based on the LPS neuroinflammation protocol.

Animals: aged male C57BL/6J mice, 18 months old.
Dosing: the aged cohort received LPS at 3 mg/kg i.p., once daily for 7 days.
Homogenisation: brain homogenate vortexed 15 s before centrifugation.
Controls: saline vehicle group included as specified in the protocol.
Perfusion and collection performed on day 8.
