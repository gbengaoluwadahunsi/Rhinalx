# Meeting note — 2024-11-12

Reviewed the pilot tolerability data (see notebook 2024-10-30): 2/8 mortality and ~18%
weight loss at LPS 1 mg/kg.

Decision: **reduce LPS induction dose to 250 µg/kg i.p.** for all main cohorts.

Rationale discussed:
- Pilot mortality/weight loss makes 1 mg/kg unworkable for a 7-day repeated schedule.
- Literature precedent: 250 µg/kg i.p. has been reported to produce reliable
  hippocampal neuroinflammation and cognitive deficits without significant mortality,
  which preserves n for behavioral and ELISA endpoints.
- Trade-off accepted: slightly milder inflammation signal in exchange for survivable,
  reproducible cohorts.

Action: G.O. to revise protocol to v3 with the new dose.
