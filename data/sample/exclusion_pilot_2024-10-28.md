# Exclusion record EXC-2024-01 (2024-10-28)

During pilot MWM acquisition, animals P-04 and P-07 failed to locate the platform in
**any** trial across all acquisition days, including cued trials with a visible platform.

Decision: **exclude P-04 and P-07 from all MWM analyses.**

Reason: consistent failure on cued (visible-platform) trials indicates a likely
**visual or sensorimotor impairment** rather than a spatial-learning deficit; including
them would confound the learning readout. Animals retained for ELISA/histology.
