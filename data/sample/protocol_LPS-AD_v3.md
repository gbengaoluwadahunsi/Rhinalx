## Animals & groups
Adult male Sprague-Dawley rats. n = 10 per group.
1. Control (vehicle)  2. LPS only  3. LPS + PVO low  4. LPS + PVO high

## Induction
LPS i.p. at **250 µg/kg**, once daily for 7 days.

## Behavioral testing
NOR: habituation + familiarization + test (24 h delay).
MWM: 5 acquisition days + probe trial.

## Endpoints
ELISA: Gal-3, p-Tau. Histology: H&E.
