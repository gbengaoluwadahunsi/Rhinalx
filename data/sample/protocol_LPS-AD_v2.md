# Protocol v2 — LPS-AD / PVO study

Changes from v1:
- Sample size increased from n = 8 to **n = 10** per group (power analysis: to detect
  a 25% reduction in Gal-3 at 80% power, alpha 0.05, n = 10 required).
- Added a NOR habituation day (open arena, no objects) 24 h before familiarization to
  reduce novelty-induced anxiety confounds.

## Animals & groups
Adult male Sprague-Dawley rats. n = 10 per group.
1. Control (vehicle)  2. LPS only  3. LPS + PVO low  4. LPS + PVO high

## Induction
LPS i.p. at **1 mg/kg**, once daily for 7 days.

## Behavioral testing
NOR: **habituation day** + familiarization + test (24 h delay).
MWM: 5 acquisition days + probe trial.

## Endpoints
ELISA: Gal-3, p-Tau. Histology: H&E.
