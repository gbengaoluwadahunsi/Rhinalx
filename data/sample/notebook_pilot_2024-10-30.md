# Pilot notebook — LPS tolerability check (2024-10-30)

Ran a small pilot (n = 8) at the v2 induction dose (LPS 1 mg/kg i.p.) to confirm
tolerability before the main cohorts.

Observations by day 4:
- 3/8 animals showed severe sickness behavior (piloerection, hunching, marked
  hypoactivity), beyond expected LPS response.
- **2 animals died** (day 3 and day 4).
- Mean body-weight loss across survivors ~18% — higher than acceptable for a
  chronic 7-day induction.

Interpretation: 1 mg/kg appears too high for a 7-day repeated i.p. schedule in this
strain; mortality and weight loss will confound behavioral endpoints and reduce n.

Action item: discuss dose reduction with supervisor before starting main cohorts.
