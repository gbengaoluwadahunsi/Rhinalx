You asked

Why did we lower the LPS dose to 0.5 mg/kg?

Claude 3 sources · reconstructed just now

The LPS dose was lowered from 1.0 to 0.5 mg/kg on 2 March 2026 to keep microglial activation within a measurable dynamic range. At 1.0 mg/kg the pilot animals showed saturating Iba1 signal that masked between-cohort differences, which defeated the study's primary comparison. The 0.5 mg/kg dose was chosen to match the effective range established for the C57BL/6 line used here.
